About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital risk, cybersecurity, and AI governance — bridging the gap between academic insight and real-world practice.

In partnership with leading UK universities, we deliver hands-on training through real-world projects in high-consequence industries — from financial services to critical infrastructure.
